Alfajores

Havanna is an Argentine company that specializes in the production of alfajores, a traditional South American sweet treat that typically consists of two cookies sandwiched together with dulce de leche (a caramel-like spread) and covered in chocolate or powdered sugar. Havanna was founded in 1948 in Mar del Plata, a coastal city in Argentina, and has since become one of the most well-known alfajor brands in the country.
